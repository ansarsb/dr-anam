Common Questions

Everything you need to know about physiotherapy

We treat a comprehensive range of conditions including sports injuries, back and neck pain, post-surgical rehabilitation, arthritis, muscle strains, frozen shoulder, sciatica, disc problems, chronic pain conditions, and mobility issues. Each treatment plan is fully customized to your specific condition and recovery goals.
Typical treatment sessions last 45-60 minutes, allowing adequate time for hands-on therapy, exercises, and education. Initial assessment appointments may take 60-90 minutes to ensure we thoroughly understand your condition and create an optimal treatment plan.
The number of sessions varies based on your specific condition, severity, and recovery goals. Most patients experience significant improvement within 6-12 sessions. We'll create a personalized timeline during your initial assessment and continuously adjust based on your progress.
No referral is required for most conditions. You can book directly with us for an initial consultation. However, some insurance plans may require a physician referral for coverage. We recommend checking with your insurance provider if you plan to use insurance benefits.
Please bring any relevant medical records, imaging results (X-rays, MRI, CT scans), a list of current medications, previous treatment history, and your insurance information if applicable. Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement and access to the affected area.
Our approach combines evidence-based techniques with personalized care. We focus on treating the root cause rather than just symptoms, use advanced manual therapy techniques, provide comprehensive patient education, and create customized home exercise programs. Our commitment to continuous progress monitoring ensures optimal outcomes.
